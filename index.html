About Us

A Place Where Curiosity Meets Care

Vedantam Play School was founded with a single belief — every child is unique and deserves an environment that honours their individuality. Our play-based curriculum blends structured learning with imaginative exploration.

Our experienced educators create warm, inclusive classrooms where children feel safe to question, create, and grow at their own pace.

  • ✔ Play-based, child-centred curriculum
  • ✔ Low child-to-teacher ratio (8:1)
  • ✔ CCTV-monitored, secure campus
  • ✔ Holistic development approach

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling your little one at Vedantam.

We welcome children from 1.5 to 5.5 years across our four programs: Playgroup (1.5–2.5 yrs), Nursery (2.5–3.5 yrs), Lower KG (3.5–4.5 yrs), and Upper KG (4.5–5.5 yrs).

School hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The administrative office remains open from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM for parent queries and admissions.

Yes! We operate GPS-tracked, air-conditioned school buses with trained female attendants on each route. Routes cover major areas across the city, and parents receive live location updates.

We follow a play-based, activity-driven curriculum aligned with early childhood development principles. It integrates language, numeracy, arts, physical education, and social-emotional learning in a balanced, holistic way.

Child safety is our top priority. We have 24/7 CCTV surveillance, biometric entry, verified staff with background checks, a no-stranger policy, and a dedicated on-campus nurse. Parents are notified instantly of any incidents.

Yes, we provide a nutritious mid-day meal and morning snack prepared fresh in our on-campus kitchen. The menu is planned by a nutritionist and rotates weekly, covering a variety of healthy, home-style dishes.

We share detailed monthly progress reports, conduct parent-teacher meetings each term, and provide a dedicated parent app with daily activity updates, photos, and teacher messages.

Required documents include: child's birth certificate, recent passport-sized photographs, parent/guardian ID proof, address proof, and immunisation records. Our team will guide you through the complete process.
